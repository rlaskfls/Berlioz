Who We Are...
Berlioz is a stage name for Jasper Edward Attlee, a jazz-house producer and multi-instrumentalist from Cape Town, South Africa, currently based in the UK. He blends elements of jazz, house, and Afro-inspired rhythms in his music, creating a unique sound described as "if Matisse made house music." His work features a mix of live instrumentation, intricate sampling, and spontaneous creativity.

Berlioz gained recognition through tracks like Jazz is for Ordinary People and his debut album Open This Wall. His live performances often feature collaborations with top musicians, combining jazz improvisation with electronic beats to create dynamic, genre-defying shows rolling stone uk, wonderlandmagazine, 303 magazine.
